Pham Van Long Phuoc

Google Scholar Github

I am a First-year PhD student at Brown University, doing Cryptography in the group of Professor Peihan Miao. Currently I focus on variants of the Private Set Intersection (PSI) problem, solved by (cleverly) borrowing tools such as Function Secret Sharing and Zero Knowledge Proofs. I did my Undergraduate study at Nanyang Technological University, where I was fortunate to be guided by Professor Wang Huaxiong and Professor Kiah Han Mao. I published a lot in Coding Theory during my Undergrad, and I'm still open to collaborations.
